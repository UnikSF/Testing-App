# Mystery Santa

A Progressive Web App (PWA) for organizing a Secret Santa gift exchange. Participants register with their name, and the organizer triggers the random assignment when ready — pairs are not revealed until the draw is launched.

**Status: not yet started**

## What it Will Do

- Participants log in with their **first name and last name** (no password / email required)
- Participants can see their own profile but not who they are assigned to until the draw
- The organizer triggers the **random assignment** manually — it does not happen on registration
- Each participant is notified of who they are gifting for after the draw
